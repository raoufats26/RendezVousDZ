🎯 PRODUCT IDEA (HUMAN LEVEL)
=================================================
RENDEZVOUSDZ is a queue & appointment system for small businesses
(barbers, clinics, salons, etc.).

There are TWO types of humans:
1) Business Owner (has an account)
2) Client / Customer (no account)

These roles must NEVER be mixed.

=================================================
👤 BUSINESS OWNER FLOW (PHASES 1–5)
=================================================
- Owner registers & logs in
- Owner owns exactly ONE business (for now)
- Owner creates their business from the dashboard
- Business stores:
  - name
  - category
  - city
  - max clients per day
- Owner dashboard shows:
  - create business form (if none exists)
  - business details (if already created)

Clients are NOT involved yet.

=================================================
👥 CLIENT FLOW (FUTURE — NOT IMPLEMENTED YET)
